The PLAN Team consists of young professionals with extensive experience in grassroots project implementation, public relations, public policy and political campaign management. The team is passionate about enacting social change through effective political and policy interventions based on research and data.

The Team:

  • Gaurav Kumar, Executive Director
    Gaurav earned Bachelors in Sociology from Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi and thereafter Masters in Sociology from Indira Gandhi National Open University, New Delhi. Gaurav has been a recipient of the Reserve Bank of India for Young Scholar Award, as part of which he worked on financial inclusion and literacy.He has been a Legislative Assistance to Member of Parliament (LAMP) Fellow with PRS Legislative Research, New Delhi, as well as a Research Associate to Members of Parliament, providing research inputs on varied legislative issues. Most recently, he worked with the Haryana CM Office as one of the inaugural Chief Minister’s Good Governance Associates, as part of which he was responsible for piloting and monitoring initiatives to improve governance, alongside monitoring the government’s flagship schemes in the district of Palwal for one full year.As a prolific writer, he has been consistently writing on various domestic and international matters for National Dailies and Magazines.
