PLAN India

Policy and Legislative Assistance Network

A Unique Initiative for Sustainable Socio-Economic Change

Who We Are:

We are a dedicated team of professionals who have built a large network of youth committed towards sustainable socio-economic change. Through public policy initiatives and direct engagement with legislators, policy makers and bureaucrats we aim to create a more responsive and proactive democracy.

  • Research Assistance

    Research support to political parties and individual legislators for legislative business, media engagements and policy development. Customized research for private member bills, parliamentary questions, debate points, reports, parliamentary committee engagements etc.

  • Constituency Development

    Needs assessment for constituencies, diagnosis of potential problems in project implementation, creation of project proposals and detailed implementation plans, technology platforms for feedback collection from constituents & stakeholders and branding of initiatives & success stories in a constituency.

  • Electoral Campaign Management

    Electoral data analytics, survey and ground intelligence collection, campaign management, insight generation for strategy building, on ground campaign management, preparation of briefs for speeches and stakeholder engagement.

  • Public Relation and Profile Management

    Creation of an effective media strategy and a powerful brand through the management of conventional, online and social media.
